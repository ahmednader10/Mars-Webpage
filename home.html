About Challenge Judging & Prizes Agenda

What?

Dell EMC Cairo is proud to organize and host its first IOT hackathon

When?

The hackathon will take place on the 22nd of September 2018

Where?

Dell EMC’s Polyom building’s Cafeteria on the ground floor

Who?

Startups employees forming teams from 3-5 participants. Team members should all belong to the same startup and a startup can only join with 1 team

Challenge

This Hackathon will challenge you to accomplish the following:
  • Develop an application on the Internet of Things (IoT) space.
  • Build and deploy a modern distributed application using best practices.
  • Deploy a distributed application using containers.
  • Practice container configuration, execution and debugging.
  • Deploy a distributed application using modern deployment technologies like Docker Swarm, Compose or Kubernetes.

Judging & Prizes

  • Each team will have to implement a set of tasks to gain maximum points throughout the challenge. [Phase 1]
  • Eligible teams (who implemented the minimal required set of tasks) will be able to join the “Final Shootout Challenge”. During this final challenge, participating teams will compete against each other to decide upon the best team solution in the Hackathon. [Phase 2]
  • Winner team is the one that collects the most points in “Phase 1” along with “Phase 2”
  • Top 3 winning teams will get cash prizes of 1500, 1200 and 900 (USD)

Agenda

  • 9 am: Hackathon event kickoff
  • 9 – 10 am: Hackathon Orientation session
  • 10 am: Coding starts
  • 8 pm: Coding ends
  • 8 – 10 pm: Judging and winners announcement
